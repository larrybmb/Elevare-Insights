What We Do

Elevare Insights specialises in community-driven market intelligence for Chinese electric vehicle manufacturers entering English-speaking markets. By building and managing authentic owner communities, we gather real-time feedback, identify product and service gaps, and provide strategic recommendations that inform product development, marketing strategies, and customer experience improvements.

Our seven-layer ecosystem model combines community management with automated intelligence gathering, AI-powered analysis, and systematic data synthesis to deliver insights that matter—from identifying £8M+ revenue risks to coordinating direct feedback with engineering teams in China.
